Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Rights, Our Commitment

Introduction

Welcome to Haricane Consultancy Services (HCS). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we manage it.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal Data: Such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you access and use our website, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: Data collected via cookies, which help us improve our services and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Provide personalized customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Enhance and optimize our website's functionality and user experience.
  • Send periodic updates, promotional offers, and newsletters (if you opt-in).
  • Monitor and analyze website usage and trends.
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience on our website. Cookies help us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Understand how you interact with our website.
  • Provide targeted advertising and content.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our website.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal data. Our measures include:

  • Using encrypted connections (SSL) to protect data transmissions.
  • Regular security audits and updates to our systems.
  • Strict internal data access controls.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data held by us. You may also:

  • Request details about the information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccuracies.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries outside of your home country. We take appropriate measures to ensure that your data remains protected, regardless of where it is processed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
